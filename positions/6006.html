Oracle SCM Technical Consultant - Order Management (OM) & PL/SQL

Experience: 4+ years | Location: Hyderabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune

Job Description

We're seeking an experienced Oracle SCM Technical Consultant to join our team. The ideal candidate will have expertise in Oracle Order Management (OM) and PL/SQL, with a strong understanding of Supply Chain Management (SCM) principles.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and implement Oracle OM solutions.
  • Configure and customize OM modules (e.g., Order Capture, Fulfillment, Pricing).
  • Develop and maintain PL/SQL scripts for data migration, integration, and reporting.
  • Integrate OM with other Oracle SCM modules (e.g., Inventory, Shipping).
  • Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues.
  • Collaborate with functional consultants and stakeholders.
  • Conduct unit testing and integration testing.
  • Provide technical support for production issues.

Requirements

  • 4+ years of experience in Oracle SCM/OM implementation.
  • Strong expertise in PL/SQL, SQL, and Oracle database.
  • In-depth knowledge of Oracle OM (Order Management) modules.
  • Experience with Oracle SCM modules (e.g., Inventory, Shipping).
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.

Desirable Qualifications

  • Oracle Certification (e.g., OCP, OCM).
  • Experience with Oracle Cloud SCM.
  • Knowledge of other programming languages (e.g., Java, Python).
  • Familiarity with Agile methodology.

Skills

  • Oracle OM configuration and customization.
  • PL/SQL development and debugging.
  • SQL and data analysis.
  • Oracle database administration.
  • Supply Chain Management principles.
